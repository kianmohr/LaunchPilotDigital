Our Story

Founded to Help Businesses Navigate the Digital World

Launch Pilot Digital was founded by Kian Mohr with a clear mission — to help businesses establish a stronger online presence through modern website design, branding, automation, and digital strategy.

With a background spanning technology, entertainment, events, marketing, and business operations, Kian has spent years working with business owners, venues, startups, and growing brands. Through these experiences, one thing became clear — many businesses struggle to navigate the digital world, often feeling overwhelmed by websites, branding, SEO, advertising, and automation.

Launch Pilot Digital exists to bridge that gap.
